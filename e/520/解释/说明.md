# v520 运行截图与解释

## 本版主题

v520 把 v519 stability metrics 与 v518 focus metrics 放到同一个只读对比报告里。它要回答一个很具体的问题：normalized full signal 是上游 stability 本来就存在，还是 focused repair 之后才出现。

边界：本版不训练模型、不修改 checkpoint、不改变任何前序实验产物。它只读取两个已归档报告并输出 contrast 证据。

## 运行命令

```powershell
python -B scripts\run_model_capability_required_term_pair_loss_alias_metric_contrast.py `
  e\519\解释\model-capability-required-term-pair-loss-alias-stability-metrics `
  e\518\解释\model-capability-required-term-pair-loss-alias-focus-metrics `
  --out-dir e\520\解释\model-capability-required-term-pair-loss-alias-metric-contrast `
  --force `
  --require-pass
```

## 关键结果

- `status=pass`
- `decision=required_term_pair_loss_alias_focus_normalized_delta_observed`
- `metric_contrast_decision=loss_alias_focus_introduced_normalized_full_signal`
- `source_metric_decision=loss_alias_stable_partial_hit`
- `focus_metric_decision=loss_alias_focus_strict_miss_normalized_support_full_signal`
- `source_normalization_gain_count=0`
- `focus_normalization_gain_count=4`
- `normalization_gain_delta=4`
- `source_stable_normalized_full=False`
- `focus_stable_normalized_full=True`
- `model_quality_claim=tiny_loss_alias_focus_formatting_sensitive_signal`

阶段对比：

| stage | strict decision | metric decision | norm full | gains |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | ---: |
| seed stability source | `loss_alias_stable_partial_hit` | `loss_alias_stable_partial_hit` | False | 0 |
| focused repair metrics | `loss_alias_focus_no_repair` | `loss_alias_focus_strict_miss_normalized_support_full_signal` | True | 4 |

结论：normalized full 不是 v515/v519 原始稳定性报告漏算出来的，而是 focused repair 阶段出现的格式敏感信号。下一步应该检查 decoding/tokenization shape，而不是直接把 normalized full 当作 strict recovery。
