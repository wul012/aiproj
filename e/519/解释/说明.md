# v519 运行截图与解释

## 本版主题

v519 把 v518 的 strict/normalized 双指标补回 `loss-alias stability` 报告。v518 已经说明 focused repair 里存在 strict miss 但 normalized hit 的现象；本版回到 v515 stability 源头，确认两 seed 的稳定性结论是否也隐藏了 normalized full signal。

边界：本版仍然不把 normalized hit 当作 strict success。它做的是稳定性报告增强与真实重跑，不改变训练目标，不扩大模型，也不修改 v513 源 alias matrix。

## 运行命令

```powershell
python -B scripts\run_model_capability_required_term_pair_loss_alias_stability.py `
  e\513\解释\model-capability-required-term-pair-continuation-span-alias-matrix `
  --out-dir e\519\解释\model-capability-required-term-pair-loss-alias-stability-metrics `
  --seeds 514 515 `
  --force `
  --require-pass
```

## 关键结果

- `status=pass`
- `decision=required_term_pair_loss_alias_stable_partial_hit`
- `loss_alias_stability_decision=loss_alias_stable_partial_hit`
- `loss_alias_stability_metric_decision=loss_alias_stable_partial_hit`
- `heldout_loss_alias_full_seed_count=1`
- `heldout_loss_alias_normalized_full_seed_count=1`
- `stable_loss_alias_full_coverage=False`
- `stable_loss_alias_normalized_full_coverage=False`
- `normalization_gain_count=0`

seed 级别结果：

| seed | strict heldout | strict full | normalized heldout | normalized full | gains |
| ---: | ---: | --- | ---: | --- | ---: |
| 514 | 3 | True | 3 | True | 0 |
| 515 | 2 | False | 2 | False | 0 |

结论：v515/v519 的 stability 源头没有隐藏 normalized full signal。v518 的 normalized full 是 focused repair 后产生的格式化拆词现象；原始 stability 仍然只能证明 stable partial。
