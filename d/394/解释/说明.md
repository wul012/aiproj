# v394 promoted seed maturity CI reasons 运行说明

## 本版主题

v394 把 promoted decision 中的 selected/global/comparison-ready handoff maturity CI regression reason counts，继续带到 promoted training scale next-cycle seed。

本版解决的问题是：next-cycle seed 负责发出下一轮 `plan_training_scale.py` 命令，但原 seed 证据只能看到 CI regression count 和 exclusion reason。v394 让 seed 在命令生成前也能展示具体 CI 回归原因分布。

## 关键改动

- `src/minigpt/promoted_training_scale_seed_review.py`
  - clean-batch review 保留 selected、full-promotion、comparison-ready reason counts。
  - seed recommendation 展示 observed reason-count detail。
- `src/minigpt/promoted_training_scale_seed_artifacts.py`
  - CSV、Markdown、HTML 展示 reason-count evidence。
- `scripts/build_promoted_training_scale_seed.py`
  - stdout 打印 selected、full、comparison-ready reason-count JSON。
- `tests/test_promoted_training_scale_seed.py`
  - fixture 和断言覆盖 nested clean-batch review、summary、rendered outputs、CLI stdout 和 recommendation。
