Scorecard Contract

LayerNew EvidenceWhy It Matters
eval_coverage component Reads design_summary.coverage_status and design_summary.comparison_status. Coverage can pass while suite design is still not comparison-ready; scorecard now caps that case.
summary Stores design coverage, design comparison, duplicate seed count, and expected-behavior completeness. Downstream tools can read prompt-design status without reopening eval suite JSON.
recommendations Warns when suite-design-complete prompt sets are needed before checkpoint claims. Turns benchmark caveats into machine-readable and human-readable review guidance.
