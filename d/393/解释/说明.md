# v393 promoted decision maturity CI reasons 运行说明

## 本版主题

v393 把 promoted comparison 中的 selected/global/comparison-ready handoff maturity CI regression reason counts，继续带到 promoted training scale baseline decision。

本版解决的问题是：baseline decision 已能排除 clean-required CI-regressed promoted inputs，但原报告更多展示 count 和 exclusion reason。v393 让 baseline 选择前的审查也能看到具体 CI 回归原因分布。

## 关键改动

- `src/minigpt/promoted_training_scale_decision.py`
  - decision row 保留 selected/global handoff maturity CI regression reason counts。
- `src/minigpt/promoted_training_scale_decision_review.py`
  - summary 聚合 selected、full-promotion、comparison-ready reason counts。
  - recommendations 展示 observed reason-count detail。
- `src/minigpt/promoted_training_scale_decision_artifacts.py`
  - CSV、Markdown、HTML 展示 reason-count evidence。
- `scripts/decide_promoted_training_scale_baseline.py`
  - stdout 打印 selected、full、comparison-ready reason-count JSON。
- `tests/test_promoted_training_scale_decision.py`
  - fixture 和断言覆盖 rejected dirty CI input、summary、rendered outputs、CLI stdout 和 recommendation。
